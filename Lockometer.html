About Broken Locks

Broken Locks and the Lockometer is your trusty review site for film and television series related to information security and privacy. Like most reviewers, we watch the films, and give our opinions on it, but unlike most reviewers, we also review the security and privacy aspects presented in the film and determine how accurate and realistic it is! This part of the discussion is backed by research and references (which will be linked to at the bottom of the reviews).

We do this so you don’t have to watch hackers fix null pointer errors on an empty line of an HTML document named "tests.py"!

If you’re into films and movies relating to privacy and security, you’re in the right place.

What is the Lockometer?

The Lockometer score is based on our opinions after watching the movie and assessing the accuracy and realism of the scenarios presented.

The Lockometer is out of 100% - the higher the score means that it is both an enjoyable film and an accurate depiction of internet security and privacy.

We weigh our ratings on the following: how enjoyable it is to the reviewer (taking up 1/3 of the total score) and how accurate or realistic the info security scenes are (taking up 2/3 of the total score).
